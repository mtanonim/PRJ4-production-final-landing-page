Meet our product

Introducing Journeymate.nl - the dating app for middle-aged singles that puts the fun back into meeting new people. With Journeymate.nl, you sign up for activities that interest you and meet like-minded individuals along the way.

Our unique badge system lets you indicate whether you’re “single and ready to mingle” or “single but not ready to mingle,” so you can find events that fit your preferences.

Whether you’re looking for a partner or just new friends, Journeymate.nl makes it easy and enjoyable to connect with others. Sign up now for early access and start your journey today!
