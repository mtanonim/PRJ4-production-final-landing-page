Vision

At JourneyMate, we envision a world where no one feels lonely, and everyone can create meaningful connections.

Mission

We strive to create a healthy community in which people feel encouraged to interact and build meaningful relationships. We aim to provide a platform for middle aged singles to find people with similar interests and create opportunities to meet in person.

Values

Creating a meaningful connection

We value the importance of human connections and believe that meaningful relationships are essential to enrich your life, making you happier and more fulfilled.

Being your true self

We value authenticity and encourage our users to embrace their true selves and fully be themselves. We believe that authenticity is the key to connecting with like-minded individuals and discovering new perspectives.

Everyone belongs

We encourage a sense of belonging by creating spaces that are welcoming and inclusive, where everyone feels seen, heard, and valued for who they truly are.

Feeling empathy

We believe that empathy is the foundation of all meaningful connections. We care deeply about creating a community where everyone feels understood and respected, no matter their background or what their journey in life is. By encouraging empathy and understanding, we can create a world where people from all backgrounds can come together, learn from one another, and forge lifelong connections.
